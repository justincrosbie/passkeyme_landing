Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing passkeys with Passkeyme.

How quickly can I integrate Passkeyme?

Most developers have passkey authentication working in under 30 minutes. Our SDK handles all the complexity, so you just need to add a few lines of code to your existing login flow.

What devices and browsers support passkeys?

Passkeys work on all modern devices and browsers, including iOS 16+, Android 9+, Chrome 67+, Safari 14+, Firefox 60+, and Edge 18+. That covers over 95% of your users!

Can I use Passkeyme alongside existing authentication?

Absolutely! Passkeyme is designed to work alongside your existing login methods. You can gradually migrate users to passkeys while maintaining backward compatibility.

What about users who don't have compatible devices?

Passkeyme gracefully falls back to traditional authentication methods for unsupported devices, ensuring all your users can still access your application.

Is Passkeyme compliant with security standards?

Yes! Passkeyme is built on FIDO2/WebAuthn standards and meets compliance requirements for PCI DSS, SOC 2, GDPR, and other major security frameworks.
